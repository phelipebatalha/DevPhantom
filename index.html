Games

Call Doggo Runner

Call Doggo Runner is an endless running game where you control superhero animals on an exciting journey through the city. The goal is to travel the greatest distance possible without losing, facing challenges and obstacles along the way. The game is divided into two main phases: Phase 1 takes place during the day, revealing a vibrant and lively city, while Phase 2 brings the thrill of the night, with illuminated and challenging urban landscapes.

Medieval Land

Medieval Land is a game that puts you in charge of your own farm, offering an immersive experience full of possibilities. Grow a variety of crops, take care of animals, and explore the vast surroundings, discovering new opportunities and resources. The game has a strong focus on building, allowing players to design and construct their own 3D structures, like houses, barns, fences, and more, customizing the farm to match their style.

ATENAS

Atenas is a 3D psychological adventure game that invites players to explore a mysterious tower, inspired by the poem Ismalia, which symbolizes the protagonist's mind and internal struggles. As the player overcomes challenges representing psychological disorders, the game's world evolves from dark shades to vibrant colors, reflecting the character's emotional recovery. The deep narrative and visual elements work together to convey a message of hope and resilience, encouraging empathy and understanding of mental battles, culminating in a journey of liberation and personal growth.

Karawara

Karawara is a mini RPG set in the Amazon rainforest, where Brazilian Indigenous culture comes through a visually rich narrative. Inspired by the beliefs, myths, and traditions of native peoples, the game combines authentic cultural elements with a Fantasy World style, creating a magical and enchanting environment. More than just entertainment, Karawara offers an educational experience, guiding players to explore Brazil's cultural roots while facing challenges that reflect serious and current issues, such as deforestation, the extinction of exotic animals, and the preservation of tribal beliefs and traditions.




Serious Games

A Gota D'Água

A Gota D'Água: Is a game developed in 48 hours during a Game Jam, inspired by the United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), with a focus on marine life protection. The game aims to raise awareness among players about the importance of preserving our seas and oceans, tackling the challenges humanity faces in protecting marine biodiversity in a fun and engaging way.

Instrumentação Cirurgica I

Game developed for the UFMG Veterinary School: Position the surgical instruments correctly on the operating table. When you are ready, click "Check" to see your results. Be careful not to overlap materials while positioning them, and pay attention to the limits.

Instrumentação Cirurgica II

Game developed for the UFMG Veterinary School: Correctly name the surgical instruments, making sure to use accents and proper spelling. When you are ready, click "Check" to see your results.

IET

The Immersion for Disorders Study: The Immersion for Disorders Study was created in collaboration with psychology and Digital Games professors at PUC Minas for the Hackathon ODS Challenge. We collected data from children and the elderly with psychological disorders through interactive tests in virtual reality. We used assessment tools such as Corsi, Nine Peg Role, and Grooved in an immersive VR environment, ensuring accurate and efficient data collection. All results are recorded and sent directly to our platform.

IOCO

Game developed for Convenção Batista Mineira: IOCO is a collection of educational mini-games designed for the Baptist Convention of Minas, aimed at children. It focuses on teaching important values in a fun and interactive way. Each mini-game is led by a unique character that represents a specific mission of the IOCO, providing a playful approach to learning and understanding these concepts.
